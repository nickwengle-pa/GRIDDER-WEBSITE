Mission Statement

Built for the
Community

The PL Gridders serves the Purchase Line School District in Commodore, PA — providing youth football and cheerleading rooted in safety, skill development, and character.

“

The purpose of the PL Gridders is to sponsor and promote youth football and youth cheerleading in the Purchase Line School District.

It is our goal to provide our students with the safest equipment as they develop their skills on the field and work their way through our programs.

Victory through character is our motto as we encourage our athletes to be leaders on the field, in the classroom, and in our community.

What We Stand For

Three Pillars of the Program

01
Safety First

We provide the safest equipment available and prioritize proper technique — ensuring every athlete can develop their skills with confidence and protection.

02
Skill Development

From first-year FLAG players to Varsity athletes, our coaches build real skills at every level and guide athletes through our programs on and off the field.

03
Character & Leadership

We develop leaders — on the field, in the classroom, and in the community. Victory through character means winning the right way and being someone teammates can count on.

Our Programs

Football & Cheerleading

Football

Tackle and flag football in the Purchase Line School District. Three competitive divisions, each designed to grow athletes at their current level and prepare them for the next step.

FLAGKindergarten – 2nd Grade · Introduction to the game
JV3rd – 4th Grade · Fundamentals & position skills
Varsity5th – 6th Grade · Advanced schemes & competitive play
Cheerleading

Our cheerleading squads perform alongside every football division, bringing energy, teamwork, and school spirit to every game day. All skill levels welcome from kindergarten through 6th grade.

FLAGKindergarten – 2nd Grade · Spirit, chants & teamwork
JV3rd – 4th Grade · Choreography & sideline performance
Varsity5th – 6th Grade · Advanced routines & game day performance
